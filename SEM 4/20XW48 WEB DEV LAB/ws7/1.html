Monkey D. Luffy
Monkey D. Luffy, also known as " Straw Hat Luffy" and commonly as "Straw Hat",[7] is the main protagonist of the manga and anime, One Piece. He is the founder and captain of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates, as well as one of its top fighters.[24] His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure left behind by the late Gol D. Roger.[25] He believes that being the Pirate King means having the most freedom in the world.

Marshall D. Teach
Marshall D. Teach, most commonly referred to by his epithet Blackbeard,[2] is the captain-turned-admiral of the Blackbeard Pirates,[8] currently one of the Four Emperors.[3] He is also the only known person in history to wield the powers of two Devil Fruits.
